Analysing the fairness of using 'points' to compare powerlifters across different weight classes

IPF GL points were created to compare the performace of powerlifters in different weight classes (as heavier powerlifters should lift more weight). If IPF GL points were a fair way to compare powerlifters across different wieght classes, then you would expect no association between a lifters body weight and their IPF GL points.
